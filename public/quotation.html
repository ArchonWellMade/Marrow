Project Summary

A bespoke, multi-page brand website for Marrow Butchery — a premium butcher and charcuterie house. The site features a hero image slider, slim left-side navigation drawer, brand story, a filterable product selection grid covering ten hand-selected cuts across Beef / Pork / Lamb / Charcuterie, five editorial information pages (Our Craft, Selection, Dry-Aging, Wholesale, Visit Us), individual product detail pages with image gallery, specifications and related items, plus newsletter capture, multi-column footer and full contact / trade-enquiry forms.

Built on Next.js 15 (App Router) with React 19. Twenty-four routes are statically pre-rendered at build time. Comprehensive responsive support covers every modern device — iMac, MacBook, iPad portrait & landscape, iPhone, iPhone SE, Android phones — with iOS safe-area awareness, touch optimisations, and graceful image-failure fallback. Source is published to GitHub and auto-deploys to Vercel on every push.

Delivered & live. Repository at github.com/ArchonWellMade/Marrow; deployed on Vercel; auto-redeploys on every push to main; first-load JavaScript ~102 kB on every page.
