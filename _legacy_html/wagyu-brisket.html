Wagyu Brisket

$52 per lb

A whole packer brisket from a Wagyu-cross herd in Yorkshire. The point and flat together. Eight to ten pounds of marbled muscle built for fourteen hours in the smoker.

We trim it for you to a quarter-inch fat cap, leaving the seam fat in place. Salt and pepper is all it needs. Cook to an internal probe-tender of 92°C, rest in a cooler for two hours.

  • OriginYorkshire, England
  • BreedWagyu x Angus
  • CutWhole packer · 4 to 5 kg
  • Trim1/4 inch fat cap, seam intact
  • CookSmoke at 110°C, 12 to 14 hours
