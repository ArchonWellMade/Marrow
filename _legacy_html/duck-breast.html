Poultry · Magret

Duck Breast

$28 per lb

Magret from Gascon ducks raised for the table, with a thick, well-developed fat cap and dark, beefy flesh. Score the skin in a tight crosshatch and start in a cold dry pan, skin-side down.

The fat renders out; the skin crisps; the flesh stays rosy. Eight to ten minutes on the skin, two on the flesh, ten of rest. Save the rendered fat for potatoes.

  • OriginGascony, France
  • BreedMulard
  • CutMagret · ~12 oz
  • ScoreCrosshatch, skin only
  • CookCold pan, skin-down, 8 min
