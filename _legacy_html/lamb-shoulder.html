Lamb Shoulder

$24 per lb

The hard-working muscle of the lamb, bone-in and well-marbled. Hours in a low oven and it falls apart at the touch of a spoon. The fat dissolves into the meat, the bone gives up its marrow.

A whole shoulder feeds four to six. Rub with garlic, anchovy and rosemary; cook covered at 130°C for five hours, then uncover for the last thirty minutes for the crust.

  • OriginYorkshire Dales, England
  • CutBone-in shoulder · ~2 kg
  • DietPasture-raised, grass-finished
  • Serves4 to 6
  • CookLow oven 130°C for 5 hours
