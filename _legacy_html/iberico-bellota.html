Iberico Bellota

$145 per lb

Hand-sliced from a single 100% pure Iberico leg, finished on acorns in the Dehesa oak forests of Extremadura. The fat is so unsaturated it melts at hand-warmth and the flavour is impossibly nutty.

We carve it to order in eighty-gram portions. Let the slices come to room temperature for ten minutes before eating; the fat needs to relax. Eat on its own; do not improve it.

  • OriginExtremadura, Spain
  • Breed100% pure Iberico
  • Cure36 months in the cellar
  • DietAcorn-finished (bellota)
  • ServeRoom temperature, hand-carved
