Pork · 14-Day Cure

Slow-Cured Belly

$28 per lb

Heritage Berkshire belly cured in our own dry rub of sea salt, raw cane sugar, juniper and black pepper for fourteen days. We rinse, dry, and rest it for another two days before it goes to the counter.

Slice thick for braises, thin for the pan. The fat layers crisp at the edges and remain silken in the middle. The cure does the seasoning for you. Taste before adding salt.

  • OriginHampshire, England
  • Cure14 days · house dry rub
  • CutBoneless belly · 1 kg piece
  • KeepRefrigerated, 10 days
  • CookLow oven 140°C for 3 hours
