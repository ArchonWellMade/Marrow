Spring Lamb

$44 per lb

Milk-fed lamb from a flock in the Welsh hills, brought in only between April and July. The meat is pale, delicately flavoured, and the fat has the faint sweetness only spring grass produces.

We offer the rack frenched, the loin boned and rolled, or the whole saddle. Roast hot and fast to medium-rare; the lamb does not want to be hurried but it does not want to be punished, either.

  • OriginBrecon Beacons, Wales
  • SeasonApril to July
  • CutRack · 8 bones, frenched
  • DietMother milk + spring grass
  • CookHot oven, rest 8 min
