Dry-Aged Ribeye

$68 per lb

Grass-fed beef from a regenerative herd in the Cotswold hills, hung on our salt-room hooks for forty-five days. The fat turns butter-yellow; the lean grows mineral and deep.

Cut bone-in to twelve ounces, with the full eye and the spinalis cap intact. The cap is where the singing happens. Cook over real heat, rest long, slice across the grain.

  • OriginCotswold Hills, England
  • BreedHereford / Aberdeen Angus
  • CutBone-in ribeye · 12 oz
  • Age45 days · dry-aged on site
  • CookHigh heat, rest 10 minutes
