Fresh Oysters

$4 each

Whitstable natives and Maldon rocks, brought to the shop alive on ice each morning. We shuck to order or send you home with the shell-knife and a glove if you would rather.

Eat them within hours. Mignonette, a wedge of lemon, a turn of black pepper, or nothing at all. The brine and the milk of the oyster are the seasoning.
